About Us

PRK LAW is a Puerto Rico based law firm dedicated to all aspects of United States immigration, including family petitions, business support, and asylum requests. The firm prides itself in forming personal relationships with clients and empowering them to make informed decisions.

Atty. Paul Kanczuzewski

A former ICE Trial Attorney, Paul grew up in Michigan where he was always inspired by metro Detroit’s diverse languages and cultures. He earned a B.A. in Spanish Literature from the University of Michigan, an M.A. in Hispanic Literature from Indiana University, and an M.S. in Finance from Walsh College of Business. As for his J.D., he graduated cum laude from the University of Puerto Rico School of Law. Paul is licensed to practice law in Puerto Rico and Florida. In other locations, his practice is limited to Immigration issues.
