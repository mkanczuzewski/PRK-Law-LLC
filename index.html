About Us


PRK LAW is a Puerto Rico based law firm dedicated to all aspects of United States immigration, including family petitions, business support, and asylum requests. The firm prides itself in forming personal relationships with clients and empowering them to make informed decisions.

Biography

Paul Kanczuzewski

Originally from Michigan, Paul began his legal career after graduating from University of Puerto Rico – Río Piedras School of Law. A member of the Puerto Rico and Florida Bars, he spent a several years defending immigrant rights in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. Paul moved back to Puerto Rico and accepted a job with the United States government, working as a Trial Attorney for Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). Subsequently, he worked a prestigious San Juan Labor and Employment law firm where he helped clients with litigation and Business Immigration. Now, Paul leads his firm and is thankful to support Bufete Gonzalez Rucci as Of Counsel.
